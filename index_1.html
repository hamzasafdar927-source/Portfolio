About

Every cut is a decision.

I'm Hamza — a freelance video editor with 6 years spent in the timeline, cutting for brands, musicians, and independent filmmakers who care about pace as much as picture. I studied editing the way most people study language: by ear, by feel, by watching a scene a hundred times until the rhythm gives itself away.

My approach starts with the story, not the software. Footage gets organized, structure gets sketched before a single cut is made, and every trim earns its place. I care about the silences as much as the sound, the frame held a beat too long, the hard cut that lands like a punch.

Based remotely, working globally — I've partnered with agencies, artists, and small studios who need someone who can own a cut from rough assembly to final grade.
